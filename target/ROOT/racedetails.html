Race Details

Teams will be made of 5 kiters. All levels of experience are welcomed to join the fun. All you need is to be able to ride up wind!

Registration is free with a team fundraising minimum of $1,000.

All registered racers receive:

Flight of the Cods race jersey

Invitation to the post-race award party with food buffet

Team fundraising page

Online fundraising tool kit

A customizable team page to collect pledges from family, friends and coworkers to sponsor your team, as well as a tool kit to help you reach these fundraising goals.

Keep in mind, prizes will also be awarded to the top fundraising team! So have fun with it, and remember its all for a great cause!
